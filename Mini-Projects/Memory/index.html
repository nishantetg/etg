Memory Card Game

How to play?

  • Click on the card.
  • Remember the card value.
  • Click on the next card.
  • If the previous card and the current card is same, you can take a next chance.
  • If card does not match, you can try your luck in next card.
  • Keep finding all the card until all cards are open.
  • Good Job! You can play again or challenge your friend to get best score.
