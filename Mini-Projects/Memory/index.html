Memory Card Game

How to start?

  • Click on input box asking for your name.
  • Enter Your Name.
  • Click on 'Play Game Now'

How to play?

  • Click on the card.
  • Remember the card value.
  • Click on the next card.
  • If the previous card and the current card is same, you can take a next chance.
  • If card does not match, you can try your luck in next card.
  • Keep finding all the card until all cards are open.
  • Good Job! You can play again or challenge your friend to get best score. Share Now  

What is Leaderboard?

  • Leaderboard is place where you can see your score.
  • Not just that, you can also see your competitor's score.
  • Leaderboard contains Rank, Player Name and Time Taken.

How to delete leaderboard's record?

  • Are you the admin of the site?
  • If yes, click on the Clear Leaderboard button at the bottom.
  • On click of the button, it will ask you the password.
  • Enter the password in the password input box and click on confirm button.
  • Boom! Previous records are deleted. Play fresh now.
